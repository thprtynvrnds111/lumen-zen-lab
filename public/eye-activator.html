What the Eye Activator actually does to your skin

The skin around the eye is 40% thinner than the rest of the face and lacks sebaceous glands — making it the first zone to show visible ageing and the most sensitive to treatment. The Eye Activator is calibrated specifically for this anatomy.

01

FAQ

Common questions

Why does the eye area need a dedicated device?

The periorbital skin is structurally different — thinner, without sebaceous glands, and more sensitive to electrical current. Standard facial devices are calibrated for thicker facial skin and can be too intense for the eye area. The Eye Activator is specifically calibrated for periorbital anatomy.

Can it actually reduce dark circles?

Dark circles have two primary causes: pooled blood/fluid (circulatory) and thin skin showing underlying vasculature (structural). The Eye Activator addresses both — thermal heat and vibration improve lymphatic drainage and circulation, while red light and microcurrent build collagen density in the overlying skin over time.

How long before I see results?

Puffiness reduction is typically noticeable from the first session due to the lymphatic drainage effect. Dark circle reduction and fine line improvement are longer-term — expect visible improvement after 3–4 weeks of daily use.

Is it safe to use near the eyes?

Yes — the device is designed for orbital rim use, not directly on the eyelid. Keep eyes closed during treatment. The red light intensity is eye-safe at the specified working distance from the skin surface.
