Your ritual

How to use your Skin Pulse

01

Prep

Cleanse and apply a water-based conductive gel or hyaluronic acid serum. The microcurrent requires a water medium — oil-based products block the current. This step determines your results.

02

Treat

Place both probes on the skin and glide slowly upward. Work zone by zone from the lower face up: jaw to cheek, cheek to temple, forehead. Hold each position for 5–8 seconds before moving.

03

Seal

Apply serum and moisturiser immediately after your session. The microcurrent temporarily elevates skin permeability — this window closes within 60 seconds of finishing.

Common questions

If it's just microcurrent, is it as effective as multi-technology devices?

For the specific outcome of muscle toning and ATP stimulation, yes — pure microcurrent is as effective as a multi-technology device delivering the same mechanism alongside others. The Skin Pulse is ideal for users who want the core clinical technology without the cost or complexity of additional modalities.

How long before I see results?

Most users notice an immediate improvement in skin radiance and a slight lift from the first session — this is from increased circulation and ATP stimulation. Structural results (firmer skin, improved contour) develop over 4–6 weeks of daily use.

What makes this different from other microcurrent devices?

The Skin Pulse uses a dual-probe design that creates a closed electrical circuit — concentrating current between the two contact points for more precise muscle targeting than paddle-style single-probe devices.

Can I use it every day?

Yes — daily use is recommended for the first 4–6 weeks. No recovery time is needed. After the initial period, 3–4 sessions per week maintains and builds on gains.
