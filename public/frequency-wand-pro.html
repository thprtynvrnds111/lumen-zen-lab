FAQ

Common questions

How is the Frequency Wand Pro different from the standard Frequency Wand?

The Pro adds radiofrequency (RF) energy — a deeper-acting modality that heats the dermis to trigger collagen contraction. This makes it more effective for pronounced skin laxity and structural lifting, where the standard Wand focuses on surface-level microcurrent and light.

What does the RF mode actually do?

RF heats collagen fibres in the dermis to 40–42°C — the threshold at which existing collagen contracts (immediate tightening) and fibroblasts are stimulated to produce new collagen (structural improvement over 4–6 weeks). It's the same mechanism used in clinic RF treatments.

How often should I use it?

Daily for the first 4–6 weeks, then 3–4 sessions per week to maintain and build on results. No recovery time needed between sessions.

Is it safe for sensitive skin?

Yes — all modes have adjustable intensity levels. Start on level 1 and increase gradually. Avoid use if pregnant, if you have metal implants in the face, a pacemaker, or active severe acne over the treatment area.
