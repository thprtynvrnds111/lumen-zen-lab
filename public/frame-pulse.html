FAQ

Common questions

Is jaw definition actually achievable with a device?

Yes — if the cause is muscle laxity rather than excess fat. The masseter and mentalis muscles respond to electrical stimulation the same way any skeletal muscle responds to training. Consistent EMS toning produces measurable improvement in jaw definition over 4–6 weeks.

Does it work for all jaw shapes?

The 3D ergonomic design fits a wide range of jaw widths and shapes. The frame is particularly effective for people noticing a softening or loss of definition along the lower face that has developed over time — the classic sign of muscle laxity rather than structural bone difference.

How often should I use it?

Daily for 10 minutes for the first 6 weeks. After that, 3–4 sessions per week maintains and builds on results. Treat it as resistance training — the cumulative effect compounds with consistency.

Can I use it while doing other things?

Yes — the hands-free design allows normal activity during the 10-minute session. Avoid talking during use as the jaw movement can disrupt electrode contact.
