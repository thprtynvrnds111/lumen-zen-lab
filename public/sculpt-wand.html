FAQ

Common questions

What does V-shaped lifting mean?

V-lifting refers to a sculpting protocol that works from the lower face upward — tightening and lifting the jawline, cheeks, and brow to produce a more defined V-shaped facial contour. The Sculpt Wand's dual-probe EMS is designed to target these zones precisely, working in sequence from chin to temple.

How is the dual-probe design different from a single probe?

A closed electrical circuit between two probes concentrates current in the muscle tissue between the contact points. This produces more targeted, localised muscle stimulation — allowing you to work on specific zones (e.g. just the cheekbone area) rather than broadly stimulating the whole face.

How often should I use it?

Daily for the first 4–6 weeks, then 3–4 sessions per week. Think of it as facial muscle training — results compound with consistency, and frequency in the early weeks establishes the baseline tone.

Can I use it around the eye area?

Avoid the immediate eye area and eyelids. The probes are designed for the broader facial zones — cheekbones, jaw, chin, forehead, and neck. For the eye area specifically, the Eye Activator is the appropriate tool.
